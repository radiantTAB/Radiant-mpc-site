RADIANT QUIKQA -- DEMO NARRATION SCRIPT
Approximately 60 seconds. Mirrors the cadence of quiklog.mp4 and quikram.mp4.

Radiant QuikQA is PyLinac-powered linac quality assurance for radiation oncology physics -- the full TG-142 program in one local web app. The TG-142 Monthly worksheet is the complete catalog with MPPG 8-b tolerances, switchable I-M-R-T or S-R-S class. Enter the deviation for each measured test, mark each functional check, and submit. QuikQA grades every row with a worst-of roll-up per group and overall, and renders a print-ready report. The Annual worksheet is the same flow at annual cadence, with an embedded TG-51 reference-dosimetry launcher -- open the photon or electron calculator, finish the absolute output cal, and transcribe the result back into the row. Every analysis module is PyLinac under the hood. Winston-Lutz takes the full M-V, k-V, or C-B-C-T image set and returns isocenter and B-B coincidence with the metrics table, the analysis plot, and a downloadable P-D-F. Radiant QuikQA. Linac QA, faster.
