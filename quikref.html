Features

What QuikRef does.

From a sprawling body of regulation to one cited answer.

One-Question Search

Ask in plain language; QuikRef retrieves the relevant passages and synthesizes a single, structured answer.

Multi-Source Corpus

NRC 10 CFR, state regulations (starting with Ohio OAC), and AAPM, ACR-ASTRO, and ASTRO guidance.

Strict Citations

Every claim ends with an inline citation to the source document, section, and page.

Conflict Flagging

Surfaces disagreements between sources instead of papering over them — and says "not addressed" rather than guessing.

Click-Through to Source

Jump straight to the cited page in the original PDF to read the passage in context.

Local-First

Runs on your machine with a local vector store; your corpus and queries stay on the workstation.

Who It's For

Made for the regulatory question.

QuikRef is built for clinical physicists and radiation safety officers who need fast, defensible answers from a large and scattered body of regulation and guidance. It replaces the keyword hunt across a dozen separate PDFs with a single synthesized answer.

Because every statement is tied to an inline citation and a click-through to the source page, an answer is never a black box — it is a starting point you can verify in seconds. The corpus is yours to curate, and everything runs locally.
