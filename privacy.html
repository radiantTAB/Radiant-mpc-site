Who we are

Radiant Medical Physics Consulting LLC ("Radiant", "we") is a single- practitioner medical-physics consulting business that develops and licenses a suite of medical-physics software tools (the "Radiant Quik Suite") for use by clinical medical physicists. Our website is radiant-mpc.com.

What we collect

  • Account information: your name, clinic or employer name, and email address, supplied when you request a trial license or sign in to the customer portal.
  • License keys: machine-bound license keys we issue you for each licensed product. These are stored in our database to support revocation and reissue.
  • Server logs: routine HTTP request logs (timestamp, source IP, requested URL, response status) collected by our edge provider (Cloudflare) and our application hosts (GitHub Pages, Fly.io, Render).
  • Session cookies: a small session cookie set during sign-in to maintain authentication state. The cookie is HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite=Lax.

What we DO NOT collect

  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or fingerprinting trackers.
  • No protected health information (PHI). Our products are designed to operate on de-identified data only; users confirm de-identification before each upload. Patient identifiers are not transmitted to our servers and are not stored.
  • No payment card data; if and when subscriptions are billed, a PCI-compliant third-party processor (e.g., Stripe) handles card details and we do not touch them.

How we use what we collect

Account information and license keys are used solely to deliver the services you've licensed: provisioning, license enforcement, software updates, and direct support communications. Server logs are used for security, fraud detection, performance triage, and capacity planning.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

How long we keep it

Account records remain while your relationship with us is active and for a reasonable period afterward (typically up to 24 months) to support reactivation, billing reconciliation, and tax retention requirements. License-key records remain for the operational life of the issued key. Edge HTTP logs are retained by our edge provider per their own retention defaults (typically 30 days).

Where it lives

All hosting is on commercial cloud infrastructure in the United States: Cloudflare (edge + Worker), GitHub Pages (static marketing site), Fly.io and Render (application hosts). The customer-portal database is a Cloudflare D1 instance.

Your rights

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, request that we correct or delete it, or revoke any license key we've issued. Contact tbernath@radiant-mpc.com and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically 30 days).

Children

Our services are intended for use by licensed clinical medical physicists. The services are not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as our practices change. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the current version.
