Do not transmit PHI through this software.

Features

What QuikPrep does.

From a binder of scattered evidence to a single, inspection-ready picture of where you stand.

Rule Library

Your state's radiation regulations pre-loaded and organized by chapter and rule — shipping with Ohio OAC 3701:1 (6 chapters, 104 rules) and built to add more jurisdictions.

Obligation Tracking

Every rule mapped to a status, an owner, and the documents that satisfy it — so you can see at a glance what's met, what's pending, and what's at risk.

Document Repository

One home for licenses, survey reports, calibration certs, policies, and training records — stored with metadata and linked to the rules they support.

Inspection Mode

Walk the inspector's checklist live, rule by rule, pulling up the supporting document for each — no scrambling through a filing cabinet mid-visit.

Gap & Readiness Reports

A PDF gap analysis of unmet or expiring obligations plus an overall readiness score — cleanly formatted and ready for the binder or leadership.

Findings & Corrective Actions

Log inspection findings and track corrective and preventive actions to closure, with owners and due dates — a documented trail between visits.

Who It's For

Made for inspection day.

QuikPrep is built for the Radiation Safety Officer, QA lead, or clinical physicist who owns regulatory readiness — the person who, when the state inspector calls, has to know in minutes whether every license, survey, calibration, and policy is current and where it lives.

It replaces the pre-inspection fire drill with a structured, always-current record: the rules that apply to you, the evidence for each, and the gaps that still need attention. Everything runs locally on a single SQLite database — no cloud, no IT project — so your regulatory record stays where you work.
