Features

What QuikQA does.

Guided Monthly and Annual QA worksheets backed by automated analysis engines — every session produces a clean, reviewable PDF report.

Monthly QA

A guided TG-142 and MPPG 8.b monthly worksheet — output and profile constancy, mechanical, imaging, and safety checks — with live tolerance grading and one consolidated report.

Annual QA

The full TG-142 / MPPG 8.b & 9.a annual program, with TG-51 photon and electron reference dosimetry — and complete chamber and electrometer catalogs — built right in.

Winston-Lutz

MV, kV, and CBCT isocenter and BB-coincidence analysis with sub-millimeter reporting.

Picket Fence

MLC leaf positional accuracy for TG-142 monthly checks, with clear pass/fail grading.

VMAT QA

DRGS and DRMLC dose-rate and MLC-speed constancy analysis from a single delivery.

TG-142 MLC Leaf-Speed

Log-based leaf-speed analysis — per-leaf RMS error, beam holds, and combined batch reports, with no portal dosimetry required.

DICOM Anonymizer & PDF Tools

Strip identifying tags to share test data safely, then merge, split, rotate, watermark, and number QA reports.

Who It's For

Made for the QA workstation.

QuikQA is built for the clinical medical physicist responsible for linear-accelerator commissioning and routine quality assurance. It consolidates the monthly and annual machine-QA workflow into guided TG-142 and MPPG 8.b / 9.a worksheets — backed by automated Winston-Lutz, Picket Fence, VMAT, and MLC leaf-speed analysis and TG-51 reference dosimetry — in a single tool that runs locally in your browser, right on the QA workstation.

Because it runs as a local web app with no Python install and no ESAPI dependency, it drops cleanly into a physics workflow without IT involvement — it is local-first, so nothing is sent to the cloud. QuikQA is distributed as an evaluation build, so every tolerance and output must be reviewed and approved by a Qualified Medical Physicist before operational use.
