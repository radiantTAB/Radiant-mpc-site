The approach

Twenty years, sharpened.

Radiant is the practice of Todd Bernath, MS — an Ohio-based medical physicist whose path into the field started on the treatment floor. B.S. in Radiation Therapy (Indiana University, 1995); practicing clinical medical physics since 2006.

Since 2021 he has provided PRN physics coverage for a regional Ohio health network, performing weekly chart checks across four Elekta Versa HD linacs. In 2024 he founded Radiant Medical Physics Consulting, LLC.
